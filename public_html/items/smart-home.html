What We Look For When Buying Smart Home Devices

Model & Age

Newer models from popular brands like Amazon, Google, and Apple typically fetch higher prices than older or less popular models.

Physical Condition

Smart home devices without scratches, cracks, or other damage will receive higher offers.

Functionality

Devices that power on and connect properly to Wi-Fi or Bluetooth are valued higher.

Factory Reset

Devices that have been factory reset and removed from previous accounts are ready for immediate resale.

Accessories

Including original power adapters, cables, and packaging can increase the value of your smart home devices.

Special Features

Devices with premium features like better speakers, displays, or sensors may be valued higher.

How to Prepare Your Smart Home Devices for Sale

  1. Factory reset your devices: Remove all personal data and settings by performing a factory reset.
  2. Remove from accounts: Unlink devices from your Amazon, Google, Apple, or other manufacturer accounts.
  3. Clean your devices: Gently clean the exterior to remove dust and fingerprints.
  4. Gather accessories: Include power adapters, cables, and original packaging if available.
  5. Test functionality: Make sure devices power on and basic functions work before bringing them in.
  6. Remove any mounted hardware: For devices like thermostats or doorbells, remove them from walls or mounting plates if possible.
