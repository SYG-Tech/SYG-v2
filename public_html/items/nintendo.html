Preparing Your Nintendo Console for Sale

Follow these steps to prepare your Nintendo console before selling it to us:

Back Up Your Data

Transfer any save data you want to keep to another console or to Nintendo Switch Online cloud storage (if subscribed).

Deregister Your Account

Remove your Nintendo Account from the console by going to System Settings > Users > [Your User] > Nintendo Account > Unlink Nintendo Account.

Factory Reset

Perform a factory reset to remove all your personal data from the device. Go to System Settings > System > Formatting Options > Initialize Console.

Remove Memory Card

Don't forget to remove any microSD cards from your Nintendo Switch.

Include Accessories

If possible, include the original dock, Joy-Cons, cables, and box to potentially increase the value.

Charge the Battery

Make sure your console has some battery charge so we can test it properly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is my Nintendo Switch worth?

The value of your Nintendo Switch depends on the model, condition, and whether you have the original accessories. Use our online estimator to get an instant quote.

Do you buy Nintendo Switch consoles with Joy-Con drift?

Yes, we buy Nintendo Switch consoles with Joy-Con drift or other issues. The value will be adjusted based on the condition, but we still offer competitive prices for devices with known issues.

How do I prepare my Nintendo Switch for trade-in?

Back up your save data, deregister your Nintendo Account from the console, perform a factory reset, remove any microSD cards, and include original accessories if possible.

Can I sell Nintendo games without the console?

Yes, we buy Nintendo games separately. We accept physical game cartridges and discs for all Nintendo systems, including Switch, Wii U, Wii, GameCube, and older consoles.
