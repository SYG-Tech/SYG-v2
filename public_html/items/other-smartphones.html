What We Look For When Buying Other Smartphones

Model & Age

Newer models from popular brands like OnePlus, Sony, and Xiaomi typically fetch higher prices than older or less popular models.

Storage Capacity

Phones with larger storage capacity (128GB+) are valued higher than those with smaller storage.

Physical Condition

Phones without cracks, scratches, or other damage will receive higher offers.

Battery Health

Phones with good battery health that hold a charge well are valued higher.

Carrier Status

Unlocked phones that can work with any carrier are typically worth more than carrier-locked devices.

Accessories

Including original chargers, cables, and packaging can increase the value of your smartphone.

How to Prepare Your Smartphone for Sale

  1. Back up your data: Make sure all your important photos, contacts, and files are backed up to the cloud or another device.
  2. Sign out of accounts: Log out of all personal accounts including Google, social media, and email.
  3. Factory reset your phone: Perform a complete factory reset to remove all personal data.
  4. Clean your phone: Gently clean the screen and body to make it look its best.
  5. Gather accessories: Include chargers, cables, and original packaging if available.
  6. Remove SIM and memory cards: Don't forget to remove your SIM card and any microSD cards before selling.
