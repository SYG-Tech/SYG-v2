What We Look For When Buying Windows Laptops

Processor & Performance

Laptops with newer Intel Core i5/i7/i9 or AMD Ryzen processors typically fetch higher prices. More RAM and faster storage also increase value.

Display Quality

Higher resolution displays (1080p, 1440p, 4K), touchscreens, and premium panel types (IPS, OLED) can significantly increase your laptop's value.

Battery Health

Laptops with good battery health that can hold a charge will be valued higher than those with degraded batteries.

Storage Capacity

Laptops with larger SSDs (256GB+) are more valuable than those with smaller storage or traditional hard drives.

Physical Condition

Laptops without cracks, dents, or significant wear will receive higher offers. Functional keyboards and touchpads are important.

Accessories

Including original chargers, cables, and packaging can increase the value of your laptop.

How to Prepare Your Windows Laptop for Sale

  1. Back up your data: Make sure all your important files, photos, and documents are backed up to an external drive or cloud service.
  2. Sign out of accounts: Log out of Microsoft account, OneDrive, browsers, and any other services.
  3. Factory reset your laptop: Perform a complete factory reset to remove all personal data.
  4. Clean your laptop: Gently clean the screen, keyboard, and exterior to make it look its best.
  5. Gather accessories: Include the original charger, any adapters, and original packaging if available.
  6. Remove password protection: Make sure the laptop is not password protected after reset.
