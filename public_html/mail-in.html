Packaging Guidelines

Use a Sturdy Box

Package your device in a sturdy cardboard box that's slightly larger than the device to allow for cushioning material.

Add Cushioning

Use bubble wrap, packing peanuts, or crumpled paper to protect your device from impacts during shipping.

Battery Safety

For devices with lithium batteries, ensure they are partially charged (30-50%) and properly secured to prevent damage.

Secure the Package

Use strong packing tape to seal all openings and seams of the box. Reinforce the bottom and top with an H-pattern.

Attach Label Properly

Securely attach the shipping label to the top of the package. Cover the label with clear tape to protect it from damage.

Data Security

Perform a factory reset on your device to remove all personal data before shipping. Disable any activation locks.

Track Your Package

Already sent your device? Enter your tracking number to check the status of your shipment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the mail-in process take?

From the time you ship your device, the entire process typically takes 5-7 business days. This includes shipping time (1-3 days), device evaluation (1 day), and payment processing (1 day after offer acceptance).

Is my device insured during shipping?

Yes, all devices shipped using our prepaid labels are fully insured up to $1,000. For higher-value items, please contact our customer service for special arrangements.

What if I don't agree with the offered price?

If you're not satisfied with our offer, we'll return your device to you free of charge. Simply decline the offer when you receive it, and we'll ship your device back within 1-2 business days.

Can I send multiple devices in one package?

Yes, you can send multiple devices in one package. Please make sure to list all devices when generating your shipping label and package them securely to prevent damage during transit.
