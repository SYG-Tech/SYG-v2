What to Bring

Your Device

Bring the device you want to sell. Make sure to remove any personal data by performing a factory reset.

Accessories (Optional)

Including chargers, cables, and original packaging can increase your device's value, but they're not required.

Valid ID

You must present a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.) to complete the transaction.

Device Passwords

Make sure you know your device passwords and have removed any activation locks (like iCloud for Apple devices).

Packaging Guidelines

Secure Packaging

Use a sturdy box with appropriate cushioning material to protect your device during transit.

Data Security

Perform a factory reset on your device to remove all personal data before shipping.

Battery Safety

For devices with lithium batteries, ensure they are partially charged (not fully depleted) and properly secured to prevent damage.

Shipping Label

Securely attach the provided shipping label to your package and keep the tracking number for reference.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the in-store process take?

The in-store evaluation typically takes 15-30 minutes, depending on the number of devices you're selling and current store traffic.

How quickly will I receive payment for mail-in devices?

Once we receive your device, evaluation takes 1-2 business days. After you accept our offer, payment is processed within 24 hours.

What if I don't agree with the offered price?

For in-store transactions, you can simply decline the offer and take your device with you. For mail-in devices, we'll return your device to you free of charge if you decline our offer.

Do you offer data wiping services?

Yes, we provide complimentary data wiping for all devices we purchase, ensuring your personal information is completely removed.
