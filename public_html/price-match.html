Our Price Match Promise

At SellYourGadgets.com, we're confident that we offer the best prices for your used electronics. That's why we back it up with our Price Match Guarantee. If you find a better offer from any legitimate competitor, we'll not only match their price but also add an extra 5% on top!

How It Works

  1. Find a Better Offer: If you receive a higher quote from another buyback service for the same device in the same condition, let us know.
  2. Show Us Proof: Provide us with documentation of the competitor's offer (screenshot, email, or written quote).
  3. Get More Cash: We'll verify the offer and beat it by adding an extra 5% to the matched price.

Eligible Competitors

Our Price Match Guarantee applies to offers from established electronics buyback services, including but not limited to:

  • SellYourGadgets.com
  • Paymore.com
  • ItsWorthMore.com
  • Gazelle
  • ecoATM
  • BuyBackWorld
  • Other major electronics buyback services

Terms & Conditions

  • The competitor's offer must be for the identical device model in the same condition.
  • The offer must be current and verifiable.
  • Price match requests must be submitted before completing your transaction.
  • We reserve the right to verify the legitimacy of any competitor offer.
  • This guarantee does not apply to limited-time promotional offers, clearance sales, or auction sites.
  • SellYourGadgets.com has the final decision on all price match requests.
