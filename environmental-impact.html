Did You Know?

E-Waste Is the Fastest Growing Waste Stream
Globally, we generate over 50 million tons of electronic waste annually, and this number is growing by 3-4% each year. Less than 20% is formally recycled.

Smartphones Contain Valuable Materials
A typical smartphone contains over 60 different elements, including precious metals like gold, silver, and platinum, as well as rare earth elements essential for technology.
