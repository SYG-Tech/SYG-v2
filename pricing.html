Factors That Affect Buyback Value

Age & Model

Newer models typically have higher buyback values. The release of new models can affect the value of older ones.

Condition

Devices in better condition receive higher offers. Scratches, dents, and functional issues can lower the value.

Storage Capacity

Higher storage capacity models (e.g., 256GB vs. 64GB) typically have higher buyback values.

Battery Health

Devices with better battery health receive higher offers. Significant battery wear can lower the value.

Carrier Lock Status

Unlocked devices typically have higher values than carrier-locked ones.

Accessories

Including original accessories and packaging can slightly increase the buyback value.
