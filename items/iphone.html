Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide the original box or accessories?

No, we'll buy your iPhone without the original box or accessories. However, including them may increase the value slightly.

What if my iPhone has a cracked screen?

We still buy iPhones with cracked screens, though it will affect the price. As long as the device powers on and is not bent or severely damaged, we'll make you an offer.

How do I remove my Apple ID before selling?

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Sign Out. Enter your Apple ID password and tap "Turn Off". Then go to Settings > General > Reset > Erase All Content and Settings.

Can I sell a carrier-locked iPhone?

Yes, we buy carrier-locked iPhones. Please let us know which carrier your iPhone is locked to during the evaluation process.
