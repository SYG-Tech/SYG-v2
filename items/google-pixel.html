Preparing Your Google Pixel for Sale

Follow these steps to prepare your Google Pixel phone before selling it to us:

Back Up Your Data

Make sure to back up all your important data, photos, and files to Google Drive or your computer.

Sign Out of Accounts

Sign out of your Google account and any other accounts you're logged into on the device.

Factory Reset

Perform a factory reset to remove all your personal data from the device. Go to Settings > System > Reset options > Erase all data (factory reset).

Remove Screen Lock

Make sure to remove any PIN, pattern, or password screen locks.

Remove SIM Card

Don't forget to remove your SIM card and any memory cards from the device.

Include Accessories

If possible, include the original charger, cable, and box to potentially increase the value.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is my Google Pixel worth?

The value of your Google Pixel depends on the model, storage capacity, condition, and whether you have the original accessories. Use our online estimator to get an instant quote.

Do you buy Google Pixel phones with cracked screens?

Yes, we buy Google Pixel phones with cracked screens or other damage. The value will be adjusted based on the extent of the damage, but we still offer competitive prices for damaged devices.

How do I prepare my Google Pixel for trade-in?

Back up your data, sign out of all accounts, perform a factory reset, remove any screen locks, and remove your SIM card. If possible, include the original charger, cable, and box.

Can I sell a Google Pixel phone that doesn't turn on?

Yes, we buy non-working Google Pixel phones. The value will be lower than a working device, but we still offer fair prices for phones that don't power on or have other significant issues.
