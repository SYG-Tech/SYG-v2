How to Prepare Your Camera Equipment for Sale

  1. Back up your photos: Make sure all your images are transferred to your computer or cloud storage.
  2. Format memory cards: After backing up, format your memory cards to remove all images.
  3. Reset camera settings: Restore your camera to factory settings to clear custom configurations.
  4. Clean your equipment: Gently clean lenses, sensors (if you know how), and camera bodies.
  5. Gather accessories: Include batteries, chargers, straps, caps, hoods, and original packaging if available.
  6. Check functionality: Make sure all features are working properly before bringing them in.
