What We Look For When Buying Samsung Tablets

Model & Age

Newer Samsung tablet models like the Galaxy Tab S9 series are valued higher than older models.

Storage Capacity

Tablets with larger storage capacity (128GB, 256GB, 512GB) are worth more than those with smaller storage.

Connectivity

Cellular (5G/LTE) models are typically worth more than Wi-Fi only models.

Screen Size & Quality

Larger screens and premium display types (AMOLED, 120Hz) increase the value of your tablet.

Physical Condition

Tablets without cracks, scratches, or dents will receive higher offers.

Accessories

Including original S Pen, chargers, cables, and packaging can increase the value of your tablet.

How to Prepare Your Samsung Tablet for Sale

  1. Back up your data: Make sure all your important files, photos, and documents are backed up to Samsung Cloud, Google Drive, or another service.
  2. Sign out of accounts: Log out of your Samsung account, Google account, and any other services.
  3. Disable Factory Reset Protection: Remove your Google account to prevent FRP lock after reset.
  4. Factory reset your tablet: Go to Settings > General Management > Reset > Factory Data Reset.
  5. Clean your tablet: Gently clean the screen and exterior to make it look its best.
  6. Gather accessories: Include the original charger, S Pen (if applicable), and original packaging if available.
