Preparing Your Smartwatch for Sale

Follow these steps to prepare your smartwatch before selling it to us:

Back Up Your Data

Make sure to back up any important data, health metrics, or settings to your phone or cloud account.

Unpair from Phone

Unpair your smartwatch from your smartphone using the companion app.

Sign Out of Accounts

Sign out of all accounts and remove any payment methods (especially for Apple Watch and Apple Pay).

Factory Reset

Perform a factory reset to remove all your personal data from the device.

Remove Activation Lock

For Apple Watch, make sure to remove the Activation Lock by unpairing properly.

Include Accessories

If possible, include the original charger, cable, and box to potentially increase the value.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is my smartwatch worth?

The value of your smartwatch depends on the brand, model, condition, and whether you have the original accessories. Use our online estimator to get an instant quote.

Do you buy smartwatches with cracked screens?

Yes, we buy smartwatches with cracked screens or other damage. The value will be adjusted based on the extent of the damage, but we still offer competitive prices for damaged devices.

How do I prepare my Apple Watch for trade-in?

For Apple Watch: Back up your data, unpair it from your iPhone (this automatically removes Activation Lock), and remove any bands that you want to keep. If possible, include the original charger and box.

Can I sell a smartwatch that doesn't turn on?

Yes, we buy non-working smartwatches. The value will be lower than a working device, but we still offer fair prices for watches that don't power on or have other significant issues.
