Device Condition Guidelines

We buy devices in ALL conditions, including new, used, broken, and for parts - as long as they don't have iCloud lock.

New

Brand new or like-new condition with no signs of use. Original packaging and accessories are a plus but not required.

Excellent

Like new condition with no visible scratches or damage. All features work perfectly, and battery health is excellent (80%+ capacity).

Good

Minor wear and tear, light scratches that don't affect the screen visibility. All features work properly, and battery health is good.

Fair

Noticeable scratches or dents, but the device is fully functional. May have some battery wear or minor functional issues.

Poor

Significant damage like cracked screens or severe dents. May have functional issues but still powers on and is identifiable.

Broken / For Parts

Non-functional or severely damaged devices. We still buy these as long as they don't have iCloud lock or similar account restrictions.

Important: We cannot accept devices with iCloud lock or other account restrictions. Please remove these before selling.

Items We Don't Accept

  • Counterfeit or replica devices
  • Devices reported as lost or stolen
  • Devices with activation locks that cannot be removed
  • Devices with water damage that won't power on
  • Devices that have been disassembled or are missing major components

Not sure if we'll accept your device? Contact us with details, and we'll let you know.
