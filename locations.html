What to Bring

Your Device

Bring the device(s) you want to sell. Make sure to remove any personal data by performing a factory reset.

Accessories (Optional)

Including chargers, cables, and original packaging can increase your device's value, but they're not required.

Valid ID

You must present a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.) to complete the transaction.

Device Passwords

Make sure you know your device passwords and have removed any activation locks (like iCloud for Apple devices).

Can't Visit a Store?

If you can't visit one of our store locations, our mail-in service is a convenient alternative. Get a free shipping label, send us your device, and receive payment within 2 business days of device receipt.
