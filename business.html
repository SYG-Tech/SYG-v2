Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum quantity to qualify for the Business Program?

Our Business Bulk Purchase Program starts at 10 devices. However, the best rates and additional services become available at higher volumes, especially for orders of 50+ devices.

How do you ensure our company data is secure?

We offer multiple levels of data security, from standard data wiping to NIST-compliant processes and DoD-level secure erasure. For highly sensitive data, we also offer physical destruction services. All processes are documented with certificates for your compliance records.

Do you offer on-site evaluation for large quantities?

Yes, for qualifying orders (typically 100+ devices), we can send our technicians to your location to evaluate devices on-site. This service is available in most major metropolitan areas and can be arranged for specific dates to minimize disruption to your operations.

What payment methods are available for businesses?

We offer several payment options for businesses including company check, ACH transfer, wire transfer, and PayPal. For enterprise clients, we can also accommodate net-30 payment terms upon approval.

Can you accommodate devices that don't power on?

Yes, we accept non-functional devices, though at reduced values. For bulk orders, we can still provide competitive rates even for devices that don't power on, as long as they're not physically damaged beyond repair.

Do you provide assistance with inventory management?

For enterprise clients (200+ devices), we offer inventory management assistance including asset tagging, detailed reporting, and integration with popular asset management systems. Our team can work with your IT department to streamline the process.
